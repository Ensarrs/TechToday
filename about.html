So who are we exactly?

TechToday is an online platform for exploring the latest news and trends in technology, from innovative gadgets to cutting-edge software, and global industry updates. Our services make it easy to stay informed by gathering articles, reviews, and analysis from various tech news sources and displaying them in one place, in a seamless experience. Whether you're in the US, UK, or anywhere else, you'll find relevant insights. No subscription fees are required to access the latest content, and there are no hidden charges after you've enjoyed the news. Stay updated effortlessly with TechToday.

TechToday is available throughout the EU, including Iceland, Norway, and Switzerland. The platform was founded by a team of seasoned tech entrepreneurs from London, Oslo, and Paris and has since grown to employ over 120 professionals. Our technical infrastructure is built on a highly reliable back-end system, utilizing Citrix/Dell shared network servers and a secure, scalable database architecture, ensuring seamless performance and a world-class user experience across all regions we serve.

In March 2023, TechToday entered a strategic partnership with Worldline, a leading European provider of payment services, to bolster its presence across the continent. This partnership enabled TechToday to further expand its reach, now serving over 3,000 corporate clients, including prominent tech expos and innovation summits, as well as 25,000 independent tech startups and innovators. In July 2022, TechToday secured $2.5 million in funding from City Index Ventures, accelerating the platform's growth and expanding its services within the global tech news industry.
